A Lifelong Learner in GIS and Cartography

I am currently pursuing a Master's of Science in Cartography and GIS, building on a Bachelor's of Arts in Environmental Science with a minor in Computer Science. My academic and professional journeys showcase my dedication to exploring and enhancing the use of spatial data.

Currently serving as a GIS & Civil Engineering Technician, I am involved in a diverse range of projects at a private engineering consulting firm. Each project offers a new opportunity to apply and expand my skills in both traditional and innovative geospatial technologies.

My journey began at a municipal water and wastewater utility, where I first engaged with the practical challenges of GIS in public service. Transitioning to a role that leverages the comprehensive suite of Esri products, including ArcGIS Pro and various ArcGIS Online tools, I continually embrace the ever-evolving landscape of GIS technology.

The constant evolution in the field of GIS and Cartography fuels my passion for this industry. There is always something new to learn, whether it's a fresh way to visualize data, a more efficient workflow, or a new application that can transform decision-making processes. This unending potential for growth and discovery is what I love most about what I do.
